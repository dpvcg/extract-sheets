Class: dpv-gdpr:A6-1-a-non-explicit-consent

consent
  • Status: proposed/accepted (created: 2019-04-04; approved: 2019-04-10; proposed by: Eva Schlehahn, Bud Bruegger, Harshvardhan J. Pandit, Rigo Wenning)
  • Superclasses: dpv:LegalBasis

  • Comment/Issue; This is also termed as 'regular' consent in the Article 29 Working Party document "Guidelines on Consent under Regulation 2016/679 (wp259rev.01)". This is the legal basis that requires consent but not at the level of being 'explicit'.

Class: dpv-gdpr:A6-1-a-explicit-consent

explicit' consent.
  • Status: proposed/accepted (created: 2019-04-04; approved: 2019-04-10; proposed by: Eva Schlehahn, Bud Bruegger, Harshvardhan J. Pandit, Rigo Wenning)
  • Superclasses: dpv:LegalBasis

  • Comment/Issue; Valid consent in this case would have requirements for being 'explicit' in addition to requirements defined by A4-11. This is also mentioned in the Article 29 Working Party document "Guidelines on Consent under Regulation 2016/679 (wp259rev.01)"
