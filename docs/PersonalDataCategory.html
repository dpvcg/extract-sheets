Class: dpv:DerivedPersonalData

Derived data is data that has not been directly provided by the DataSubject, but has been derived from other collected or externally obtained data.
  • Status: accepted (created: 2019-05-07; approved: 2019-05-07; proposed by: Elmar; Harshvardhan J. Pandit, Fajar)
  • Superclasses: dpv:PersonalDataCategory

Class: dpv:SpecialCategoryPersonalData

Special category or personal data as per GDPR Art. 9 (1)
  • Status: accepted (created: 2019-04-04; approved: 2019-05-07; proposed by: Elmar; Harshvardhan J. Pandit, Fajar)
  • Superclasses: dpv:PersonalDataCategory

  • Comment/Issue; trade union membership, which is explicitly included in the taxative listing in GDPR Art. 9 (1), is not covered yet.
